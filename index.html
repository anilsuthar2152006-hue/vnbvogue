Our Story

A Sanctuary of Refined Artistry

At R&A Unisex Salon, we believe your hair is your crown — and it deserves world-class care. Founded on the principles of timeless elegance and modern technique, our salon offers bespoke colour services, beautiful hairdressing, transformative makeup, and meticulous nail artistry in an environment crafted for total relaxation.

Located in Mahavir Enclave, New Delhi, we bring premium beauty expertise to every client — from everyday styling to grand bridal moments.
